Join the lab

We welcome prospective PhD students, MSc students, and visiting scholars with interests in computer vision, graphics, NLP and large language models, and trustworthy AI. Strong programming skills and research curiosity matter most.

PhD applicants
Apply through the NUS SoC PhD programme, and email A/Prof Huang with your CV, transcript, and research interests.
MSc students
Current NUS MSc students interested in a project or thesis with the lab are encouraged to reach out with a short statement of interest.
Visiting scholars
We regularly host visiting PhD students and researchers. Include your home institution, supervisor, and intended visit dates when you write.
